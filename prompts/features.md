## Services Provided


### 1 Gate Module (Security & Visitor Management)

The **flagship product** and original use-case:

#### For Residents:
- **Pre-approvals** — Approve expected visitors in advance with a unique code
- **Spot entry approvals** — Real-time notifications when unexpected visitors arrive
- **Guest invitations** — Generate digital invites with QR codes
- **Recurring invites** — Permanent access for regular visitors
- **Leave-at-gate** — Instruct guards to collect parcels/items at the gate
- **Daily help management** — Track attendance, timing, and payments for domestic workers
- **Child safety alerts** — Notifications when children enter/exit the premises
- **Visitor overstay alerts** — Get notified if a visitor stays beyond expected time
- **Panic button** — Emergency alert feature
- **Emergency contacts** — Quick access to local services

#### For Security Guards:
- **Group visitor entry** — Handle bulk entries efficiently
- **Frequent visitor handling** — Quick processing for regular visitors
- **Photo capture** — Photograph visitors at entry
- **Multi-tier check-in** — Vehicle + person verification
- **Guard patrolling** — Digital patrolling checkpoints and logs
- **Guard-to-guard calling** — In-app communication between guards
- **Voice command entry** — Hands-free visitor processing
- **Offline mode** — Works without internet connectivity
- **Multilingual support** — Interface in multiple Indian languages
- **E-intercom** — Digital intercom to residents

#### For Admins:
- **Visitor overstay alerts**
- **Utility vehicle tracking**
- **Material gatepass** — Track materials leaving/entering the society
- **Guard patrolling reports**
- **App & IVR authentication**

### 4 Amenities Module

Manage shared community facilities:

- **Amenity booking** — Clubhouse, gym, pool, party halls, tennis courts
- **Capacity and usage control** — Prevent overbooking
- **Slot management** with start-time customization and cool-down periods
- **Differential pricing** — Different rates for different amenity categories
- **Companion management** — Track guests at amenities
- **Integrated check-in/check-out**
- **Cancellation management** with limits and charges
- **BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later)** option for bookings
- **Maintenance closure** calendar and holiday management
- **Defaulter blocking** — Block amenity access for maintenance defaulters
- **2.4M+ amenity bookings managed annually**
