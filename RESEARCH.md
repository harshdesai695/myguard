## 1. What is MyGate?

**MyGate** is India's largest **gated community management platform** — a mobile-first application designed to digitize and streamline every aspect of life within residential housing societies (apartments, gated communities, townships).

- **Founded:** 2016, Bangalore, India
- **Founders:** Vijay Arisetty (Chairman, ex-IAF helicopter pilot, ISB alumnus), Abhishek Kumar (CEO, IIT Kanpur + IIM Ahmedabad), Shreyans Daga (CPO, IIT Guwahati + ISB)
- **Parent Company:** Vivish Technologies Private Limited
- **Headquarters:** 12, 17th Cross, Sector 7, HSR Layout, Bengaluru 560102
- **CIN:** U66220KA2016PTC085219
- **Scale (as of 2025-26):**
  - **27,000+** societies/communities
  - **5 million+** homes
  - **50+ cities** across India (metros + tier-2 towns)
  - **100,000+** RWAs (Resident Welfare Associations)
  - **1,550+** employees
  - **74-82% market share** in the gated community management category (Redseer 2026 report)

MyGate connects **all stakeholders** of a gated community — residents, security guards, RWA management committees, facility managers, vendors, and daily help (domestic workers) — into a single digital ecosystem.

### Core Problem Solved

Indian housing societies traditionally depend on manual registers, paper logs, phone calls, and physical cash for security, visitor management, maintenance billing, and community governance. MyGate digitizes all of this, providing:

- **Security & Access Control** — Digital visitor approvals, guard management, vehicle tracking
- **Community Governance** — Accounting, billing, complaint management, communication
- **Resident Convenience** — Delivery tracking, home services, amenity booking, marketplace

## 3. How Does MyGate Generate Revenue?

MyGate operates a **multi-layered revenue model** with several distinct streams:

### 3.1 SaaS Subscription Fees (B2B — Primary Revenue)

- **Target Customers:** RWAs (Resident Welfare Associations), Facility Management companies
- **Model:** Monthly/annual subscription charged to housing societies for access to the platform
- **Pricing Tiers:** Based on society size (number of units/flats), features selected (Gate module, ERP module, etc.)
- **Revenue Driver:** Onboarding more societies = more recurring subscription revenue
- This is the **core revenue engine** — revenue growth is directly tied to the number of onboarded communities

### 3.2 Advertising Platform (B2B — High-Growth Revenue)

MyGate has built a **premium advertising platform** that lets brands reach affluent, gated-community audiences:

- **Digital Ad Formats:**
  - **Feed Cards** — Banners/videos in the community social feed
  - **Notice Pulse** — Push alerts to residents
  - **Approval Cards** — Full-screen ads shown after a resident approves a visitor, parcel, or cab
  - **Masthead** — Prominent placement on the homescreen (viewed 3-5 times/day)
- **Offline Ad Formats:**
  - On-ground activations within societies
  - Standees, banners, and event sponsorships in community common areas
- **Clients:** 2,000+ brands including Amazon, Zomato, Swiggy, Myntra, L'Oréal, HUL, Google, Audi, Tanishq, Ajio
- **Value Proposition:** 47% DAU:MAU ratio = high-frequency daily usage; access to premium households behind gates
- **Privacy Consideration:** This model has faced scrutiny from users concerned about data being used for targeted advertising

### 3.3 Transaction Fees / Payment Processing

- MyGate processes **₹90 Billion+** in invoice value annually through its platform
- Charges a **small transaction fee** on maintenance dues, facility bookings, and other payments processed via the app
- Integrated payment gateway supporting multiple payment modes

### 3.4 Hardware Sales (Smart Locks & Devices)

- **MyGate Smart Locks** — Smart lock devices sold to communities and individual residents
- Available through their own e-commerce store (shop.mygate.com)
- Extends the ecosystem from gate-level security to door-level security
- Represents the **IoT/hardware revenue stream**

### 3.5 Home Services Marketplace (Commission/Lead-Gen)

- Connects residents with service providers (plumbers, electricians, cleaners, etc.)
- Revenue via **commission on bookings** or lead-generation fees from service providers
- Business grew 50% month-over-month as reported in Dec 2021

### 3.6 Real Estate / Property Marketplace (MyGate Homes)

- Launched in 2021 — enables residents to buy, sell, and rent properties
- Revenue via **listing fees, featured placements, and lead-generation** for real estate agents/builders
- Expanded to include rental agreements, legal assistance, and property management

### 3.7 Community Commerce (MyGate Exclusives)

- Curated e-commerce within the app featuring household and essential products
- Revenue via **marketplace commissions and direct sales**
- Acquired MyCommunity Genie in 2021 to bolster this segment

### 3.8 Strategic Partnerships & Integrations

- Partnerships with companies like **Urban Company** (home services), **Acko** (insurance), **DriveU** (car services)
- These partners invested ₹100 Cr in MyGate (2022) to gain access to the community user base
- Revenue via **referral fees, revenue sharing, and co-branded services**

```
├── Home Services Marketplace
├── Property Marketplace
├── Community Commerce (e-store)
├── Smart Hardware (locks, boom barriers)
└── Partner Integrations
```

### Key Architectural Considerations

1. **Multi-tenant SaaS** — Each society is a tenant with isolated data
2. **Offline-first guard app** — Guards often have poor connectivity
3. **Real-time notifications** — Visitor approvals must be instant (FCM/APNs)
4. **Role-based access control** — Residents, guards, admins, committee members all have different permissions
5. **Payment gateway integration** — Handle maintenance dues, booking fees
6. **Data privacy & security** — Number masking, access logs, GDPR-style controls
7. **Scalability** — MyGate processes 1.2B+ entries/year; design for scale
8. **Multi-language support** — Critical for guard adoption across India
9. **Hardware integration APIs** — Boom barriers, ANPR cameras, smart locks

### Revenue Model Blueprint

1. **Start with SaaS subscriptions** to RWAs (per-unit monthly pricing)
2. **Add transaction fees** on payment processing
3. **Build advertising platform** once you have 1000+ societies (premium audience)
4. **Layer on marketplace commissions** (home services, property, commerce)
5. **Introduce hardware products** (smart locks, access devices) for additional margin

### Critical Success Factors

- **Guard adoption** is the hardest problem — invest heavily in training, UX, and offline support
- **RWA committee buy-in** is the sales bottleneck — target the management committee, not individual residents
- **Network effects** kick in when residents start depending on the app for daily tasks (deliveries, help attendance)
- **Stickiness** comes from becoming the default communication + payment layer for the society
